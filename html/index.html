About Us
NTUC FairPrice was first established on 22 July 1973 as NTUC Welcome Supermarket in Toa Payoh, to solve the rising oil and daily prices then due to inflation. Then prime minister Lee Kuan Yew opened the first supermarket at Block 192, Toa Payoh Lorong 4, and it was the first of its kind. This website is built for people in Singapore to order products from Fairprice and to find out more about FairPrice. They would also be able to see what promotions FairPrice has which would entice them to place an order.
